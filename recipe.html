The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Open you jar of jelly
  2. Open your favorite jar peanut butter
  3. Grab yourself Two slices of bread
  4. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  5. With the butter knife, spread an inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  6. On the right side, spread an inch layer of jelly.
  7. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  8. With the knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  9. Enjoy your sandwich!
